Purpose. Cardinal Inspections is the home for every roof inspection report produced by Cardinal Roofing & Renovations. Each report is built from our standard branded template — covering exterior elevations, aerial overview, roof surface, penetrations and flashings, chimney, attic and ventilation, findings by priority, and recommendations — so that every client receives a consistent, professional, photo-documented record of their roof's condition. From this page you can create a new inspection, edit and print any report to PDF, and track whether — and when — each report was sent to the client.
